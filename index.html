About This Recipe

This is a simple guide on how to make Nigerian Jollof Rice, created as part of the July 2025 HTML5 assignment. The structure and layout follow accessibility and SEO best practices as requested.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 cup tomato and pepper blend
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon thyme and curry
  • 2 cups chicken or beef broth

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and sauté chopped onions.
  2. Add the tomato-pepper mix and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Pour in broth and seasonings, then bring to a boil.
  4. Add washed rice, stir, and cover.
  5. Cook on low heat until rice is soft and fully cooked.

Chef's Tip

To get that classic smoky "party rice" flavor, let the rice burn slightly at the bottom of the pot before turning off the heat.
